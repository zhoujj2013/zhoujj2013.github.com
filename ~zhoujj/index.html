About

Zhou Jiajian is a Bioinformatic Analyst in Microbial Research Group, BGI shenzhen and is a M.Sc student of CUHK-BGI Innovation Institite of Trans-Omics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. He was a Bioinformatic Analyst in DEPT. of Social Evolution Research, BGI in 2010-2011. He got his B.Sc degree in Biotechnoloy from South China Agricultural University, GuangZhou, China. Now, his research is about bioinformatics, microbial genomics and NGS method for clinical.

Research Interests

  • NGS method for clinical: find biomarks and apply to disease diagnosis.
